Paper Wings World ("we", "us", or "our") is a product of ooabisabi LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

- **Account & Authentication Data**: When you sign up with email or use anonymous sign-in, we collect your email address (if provided), a unique user identifier, and authentication tokens via Supabase.
- **Game Progress Data**: Your folding progress, flight distances, best scores, unlocked regions, and mastery badges. This data is stored locally and synchronized to the cloud when you are signed in.
- **Purchase & Subscription Data**: Information about in-app purchases (product identifiers and purchase receipts) processed through Apple App Store or Google Play via RevenueCat. We do not store full payment details.
- **Device & Usage Information**: Device type, operating system version, and basic performance data for improving the app (future analytics).

We do **not** collect precise location, photos, contacts, or any data from your camera or microphone.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide core features (folding tutorials, flight simulation, progress tracking, cloud sync).
- To enable and restore your in-app purchases.
- To improve the app, fix bugs, and optimize performance.
- To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

## 3. Sharing of Information

We share information only with:
- Supabase (authentication and database hosting)
- RevenueCat / Apple / Google (in-app purchase processing)
- Future analytics or crash reporting services (to be disclosed when added)

We do not sell your personal information.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Paper Wings World is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for children. We comply with applicable children's privacy laws (including COPPA in the US). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is necessary for the core educational experience. Parents may contact us to review or delete a child's data.

## 5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures through our backend providers (Supabase). All data in transit is encrypted. However, no system is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

- You can sign out or delete your account from the Settings screen.
- You can restore purchases at any time.
- You may request deletion of your account and associated progress by contacting us (see below).
- Cloud data is deleted when your account is removed.

## 7. Third-Party Services

- Supabase (https://supabase.com) — auth & database
- RevenueCat (https://www.revenuecat.com) — subscription & purchase management
- Unity Technologies (engine & services)

Their privacy policies apply in addition to this one.
