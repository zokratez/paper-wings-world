# v1.0 MVP Scope Lock (Exactly 8 Planes + 3 Regions)

**Approved**: 2026-05-19 via explicit user confirmation.

This document is the single source of truth. Anything not listed here is explicitly out of scope for v1.0.

## Planes (Exactly 8)

See [Paper-Airplanes.md](../Game-Features/Paper-Airplanes.md) for the full locked list and details.

- Free (3): Classic Dart, Glider (Nakamichi), Ring/Annular
- Paid Unlock (5): Nakamura Lock, Canard, Delta Wing, Twin-Fin Stabilizer, Scale-Model Style

## Flight Regions (Exactly 3)

1. **Grand Canyon, Arizona, USA** — dramatic relief, multiple altitude bands, canyon flying
2. **Mt. Fuji foothills + Japanese countryside** — forests, small shrines, rural coast
3. **Scottish Highlands or Norwegian fjord coast** — cliffs, lighthouses, islands, weather

**Rationale for these three**: Strong visual identity, variety of terrain types, good public data availability, instantly recognizable to families.

## Modes

- Peaceful Exploration (default, always available)
- Challenge (wind gusts, thermals, later tempests) — unlocked after first 3 planes

## Monetization

- Free tier: 3 planes + Grand Canyon + Peaceful mode
- One-time unlock or subscription: remaining planes + 2 regions + Challenge mode + printables + ad-free
- No lifetime deals

## Out of Scope for v1.0 (Explicitly Deferred)

- Any planes beyond the 8 listed
- Any regions beyond the 3 listed
- AR / camera overlay
- Multiplayer, sharing, or social features
- User-generated content
- Full-planet high-fidelity photorealistic streaming (see Tech-Decisions)
- Voice narration (text only; TTS optional later)
- Classroom / teacher dashboard
- Any paid 3D data service (Cesium ion paid tiers, etc.) until revenue

## Success Metrics (Device + User Verified)

- A 9–12 year old can complete one full folding tutorial + first satisfying flight in < 8 minutes on an iPad without adult help.
- The three regions feel distinctly beautiful and "real" at both high and low altitude.
- Parents report the app feels premium and educational, not gimmicky.
- Zero crashes or frustrating "you died" moments in Peaceful mode.
