# Paper Wings World — Vision

**Tagline**: Fold real paper airplanes. Fly them over the real world.

A delightful, educational, family-friendly mobile experience that combines the timeless joy of paper airplane folding with the wonder of exploring our actual planet in 3D.

## Core Experience Loop

1. **Learn** — Choose a real paper airplane design from around the world.
2. **Fold** — Follow beautiful, interactive, step-by-step 3D animated tutorials with touch-to-fold interaction, realistic paper physics, and helpful hints.
3. **Print** — Generate a printable PDF template (A4/Letter) with clear fold lines and annotations.
4. **Launch** — Take your folded plane into a realistic 3D globe and fly it freely.
5. **Explore** — Soar over mountains, canyons, forests, coastlines, temples, and famous landmarks using real terrain data. Peaceful mode for pure joy; Challenge mode for wind, thermals, and weather when ready.

## Why This Matters

- Paper airplanes are one of the purest, lowest-friction creative acts for children and adults.
- Most kids (and many adults) have never seen how real, well-designed paper planes actually fly when given proper technique.
- Combining it with a real 3D Earth turns a 30-second craft activity into an hours-long exploration and learning journey.
- Strong educational angle: aerodynamics, geography, world cultures, patience, and fine motor skills — all without feeling like "school."

## Target Audience

- Primary: Kids 7–14 (with parents)
- Secondary: Curious adults, educators, grandparents who fold with grandkids
- Tertiary: Aviation enthusiasts and paper-craft hobbyists

**Positioning**: "The most beautiful paper airplane app ever made — and the only one where your plane can fly over the Grand Canyon, Mt. Fuji, and the Scottish coast."

## Emotional Tone

- Joyful, calm, slightly magical
- Never frustrating (especially for young players)
- Pride in both the folding craft and the long, graceful flights
- "I made this and now I'm flying it over a real place"

## v1.0 Success Criteria (Locked)

- A 9–12 year old can independently complete one folding tutorial and enjoy a satisfying first flight in under 8 minutes.
- The three starting regions feel distinctly beautiful and real.
- Parents say "this is the app I wish existed when I was a kid."
- Zero paywall friction for the free tier; clear, honest value for the paid unlock.

## Non-Goals (v1.0)

- No AR
- No multiplayer or sharing
- No user-generated planes
- No global photorealistic streaming (see Tech Decisions)
- No voice acting (text + optional TTS later)

## Later Chapters (Post-Revenue)

- Additional cultural planes and regions (Swiss Alps, New Zealand, Patagonia, Iceland, etc.)
- More advanced aerodynamics challenges
- Teacher / classroom mode with printable lesson plans
- Community plane designs (curated)
