# Paper Airplanes — v1.0 Locked List (Exactly 8)

**Scope Lock**: These are the only planes that will ship in v1.0. No additions without explicit approval.

## Free Tier (3 planes — always available)

### 1. Classic Dart
- **Origin**: Universal / schoolyard classic
- **Difficulty**: 1/5 (2–3 minutes)
- **Flight Character**: Fast, pointy, good speed, moderate stability
- **Key Folds**: Simple fuselage, basic wings, tail fin
- **Educational Note**: "The dart is the fastest to fold and the fastest to fly. Great for learning how a sharp leading edge cuts through air."

### 2. Glider (Nakamichi-style Long-Distance)
- **Origin**: Modern Japanese design lineage (Nakamichi)
- **Difficulty**: 2/5 (4–6 minutes)
- **Flight Character**: Long, graceful glides, excellent for distance
- **Key Folds**: Wide wings, slight dihedral, locked rear
- **Educational Note**: "Wider wings and a gentle curve create lift. This plane trades speed for staying in the air much longer."

### 3. Ring / Annular (Hoop)
- **Origin**: Modern novelty / science experiment
- **Difficulty**: 2/5 (3–5 minutes)
- **Flight Character**: Extremely stable, slow, almost hovers in light wind
- **Key Folds**: Two rings connected by a tube
- **Educational Note**: "The ring shape creates a vortex that keeps it amazingly stable. One of the most forgiving designs for beginners."

## Paid Unlock (5 planes)

### 4. Nakamura Lock
- **Origin**: Japanese (Nakamura)
- **Difficulty**: 3/5 (6–8 minutes)
- **Flight Character**: Outstanding stability, forgiving, great for outdoor wind
- **Key Folds**: Intricate locking mechanism at the nose and wings
- **Educational Note**: "The lock at the front keeps everything rigid. This is one of the best all-around flyers in the world."

### 5. Canard / Forward-Wing (French lineage)
- **Origin**: French paper aviation experiments
- **Difficulty**: 4/5 (8–10 minutes)
- **Flight Character**: Unusual but excellent glide when folded correctly; counter-intuitive look
- **Key Folds**: Small forward wings + large main wings
- **Educational Note**: "The little wings in front (canards) help control pitch. Real jets and some birds use the same idea."

### 6. Delta Wing (Modern Sport)
- **Origin**: Modern sport / competition
- **Difficulty**: 3/5 (5–7 minutes)
- **Flight Character**: Fast, agile, pointy, loves speed
- **Key Folds**: Large triangular wings, reinforced fuselage
- **Educational Note**: "Delta wings are used on supersonic jets. They handle high speed beautifully but need a strong, straight throw."

### 7. Twin-Fin Stabilizer
- **Origin**: American / competition circuit
- **Difficulty**: 3/5 (6–8 minutes)
- **Flight Character**: Very stable, predictable, excellent in light wind
- **Key Folds**: Two vertical stabilizers + precise wing folds
- **Educational Note**: "Two fins give it the stability of a real cargo plane. One of the most reliable distance flyers."

### 8. Scale-Model Style (Complex)
- **Origin**: Advanced origami-inspired paper aviation
- **Difficulty**: 5/5 (12–16 minutes)
- **Flight Character**: Beautiful, slower, more "real plane" look and feel
- **Key Folds**: Many precise creases, layered wings, detailed tail
- **Educational Note**: "This is the closest you can get to a real model airplane using only paper. Every fold matters."
